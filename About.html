About YBA's BSL Club

Who is behind the club?

The BSL Club is run by students and staff at YBA who are passionate about British Sign Language. Whether you are a complete beginner or already know some signs, we are here to help everyone learn together!

Club Leaders: Theo Ogburn, Geraint Venn-Jones, Emily Plevin, and Mrs. Bourhill (English E4)

What do we actually do?

In our weekly sessions, we keep things fun and interactive. Here is a look at what happens when you come along:

Activity / Workshop What it involves
Fingerspelling Learning the BSL alphabet so you can spell names and places.
Deaf Culture Learning how to be respectful and helpful when meeting Deaf people.
Greeting Workshops Interactive sessions on introducing yourself and basic conversation.
Song Signing A fun workshop where we learn to sign along to popular songs.

Our Goal 🎯

Our goal is to create a friendly space where YBA students can pick up a new skill that actually makes a difference in the real world. We want to see people signing in the corridors!
