Welcome to YBA's BSL Club!

Welcome to our British Sign Language Club! We aim to teach students how to communicate and sign with the Deaf community. You may be asking yourself, "Why should I learn Sign Language?" There are many great reasons to learn BSL:

  • To communicate and sign with Deaf people.
  • To prepare for a future career, such as an interpreter or a Special Educational Needs (SEN) teacher.
  • To learn it for fun—which is a perfect reason to join!
  • To master a new language that allows you to connect with a wide variety of people in a unique way.

Did You Know? 💡

  • BSL is its own language with its own grammar—it’s not just English with hands!
  • Facial expressions are just as important as hand signs.
  • Learning BSL helps make our school a more inclusive place for everyone.
