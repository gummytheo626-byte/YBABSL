Welcome to YBA's BSL Club!

Empowering students to communicate and connect with the Deaf community.

Why Learn British Sign Language?

  • Communicate effectively with Deaf individuals.
  • Prepare for future careers in interpretation or SEN teaching.
  • Master a unique language while having fun with friends!

Did You Know? 💡

  • BSL has its own unique grammar and structure.
  • Facial expressions are a vital part of communication.
  • Your participation helps create a more inclusive school.
